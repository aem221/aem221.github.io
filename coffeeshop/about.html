History of dream Beans Coffee

Paris, France 2013

We were walking all around Montmarte and passing by the studios of Salvador Dali, Pablo Picasso, and Vincent van Gogh.
We had walked up the hill to Sacre Coeur and we were ready to have a latte.
All of the cafes were crowded or closed. At that moment we chose to open dream Bean Caffe in this beautfil part of Paris.

She was free in her wildness. She was a wandress, a drop of free water. She belonged to no man and to no city. - Roman Payne, The Wanderess

This reminded us of the movie Amelie. Right then, we knew this was the place to open our coffee shop.
